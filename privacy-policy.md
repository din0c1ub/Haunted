# Privacy Policy for Haunted

Last updated: April 2026

Haunted ("the App") is developed by Dinoclub. This policy describes how user data is handled.

## Data Collection

The App itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All sound preferences and presets are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage (UserDefaults).

## Third-Party Services

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data as described in Google's privacy policy (https://policies.google.com/privacy). This may include:

- Advertising identifiers (IDFA)
- Device information
- Usage data for ad personalization

You can opt out of personalized ads through your device's Settings > Privacy > Tracking.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
